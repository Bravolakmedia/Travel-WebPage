A TRIP TO OSOGBO

Osogbo is an ancient city in Osun State in South-Western part of Nigeria.
It is the home of the famous Osun Shrine, which is a world acclaimed tourist centre that
brings a lot of people from all over the world to Osogbo every August of the year.
Osogbo is also famous for artefacts and production of dyes of diverse importance.
The city is a nice place to visit for different reasons ranging from


  • Cultural Richness
  • Tourist Attractive Centres
  • Educational Importance
  • Political Importance

When next you travel to Osogbo for political, cultural, education or any other reason find time to
visit the following centres.

  1. Osun Groove: The natural forrest that houses the Osun shrine where Osun worshipers worship
  2. Ojubo Ikun: A popular restaurant that sells ancient local foods with ancient drinks e.g cool palm-wine.
  3. Freedom Park: The centre is named after Nelson Mendalla, a freedom fighter as a symbol of democracy.
  4. 264-Dam: This is an artificial dam constructed by white contractors. It is believed to be 264miles deep.
